Why this logo?

The QTA logo is a green heart with a heartbeat behind it. The heart represents the care and attention given to patients, while the heartbeat represents the efficiency and effectiveness of the system. The logo is a symbol of hope and optimism, and represents the belief that QTA can help improve healthcare and the quality of life for patients.

Do more, wait less

The slogan "Do more, wait less" encapsulates the essence of our request management system, QTA. With QTA, users can accomplish a greater number of tasks in less time, boosting productivity and efficiency. Furthermore, the wait time for responses is significantly reduced, providing a more agile and satisfying experience for users.
